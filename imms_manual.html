Version: 1.0.0 | Format: Digital Document HTML

1. Authentication & Roles

The IMMS platform operates on a strict multi-tier role-based access system:

  • Admin: Complete system visibility. Can manage users, create properties, assign work orders, and oversee spatial assets.
  • Engineer / Technician: Can register and map assets, initiate field transfers, and inspect property registries.
  • Vendor: Dedicated access to view available Work Orders. Can exclusively bid securely on operational maintenance tasks.

2. Property GIS Mapping

Properties form the foundational registry of the IMMS architecture. Ensure precise Latitude and Longitude coordinates are configured to automatically trigger spatial mapping across the system.

3. Asset Lifecycle & Financial Depreciation

When registering an asset, you must link it directly to an active Property zone.

The platform mathematically calculates an asset's Value Drop strictly after the 1st year of purchase dynamically against the current date based on standard depreciation inputs.

4. Secured Internal Transfers

Assets can be digitally transferred between verified properties. A robust historical transfer ledger tracks the authorizing personnel and securely timestamps every movement for complete auditing capability.

5. Operational Bidding (Work Orders)

Administrators broadcast targeted technical tasks against specific sub-assets via the Work Orders module. Third-party maintenance personnel (Vendors) log in uniquely to review task scopes and submit competitive financial bids.

Security Notice: Invoices (PDF/IMG files) accompanying high-value assets are uploaded directly via an exclusive raw-bypass protocol towards regional Cloud CDN servers. Always ensure your PDFs are verified before linking.
